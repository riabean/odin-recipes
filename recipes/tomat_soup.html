Description

Tomato soup is the ultimate simple comfort food. It's warm, filling, and takes very little effort to make from scratch — much better than anything from a can.

It works great as a starter or as a light meal on its own, especially with a slice of crusty bread on the side.

Cooking Directions

Ingredients

  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500ml vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil
  • Fresh basil to serve

Steps

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute. Pour in crushed tomatoes and broth.
  3. Add sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Blend until smooth with a hand blender.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve with fresh basil and bread.
